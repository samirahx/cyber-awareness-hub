Common Cyber Threats

Phishing

Fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive info through fake emails or websites.

Malware

Malicious software that can damage or take control of your device.

Ransomware

Software that locks your files and demands payment to unlock them.

Identity Theft

Stealing your personal info to impersonate or commit fraud.

Tips to Stay Safe

  • Use strong and unique passwords for every account.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible.
  • Keep your software and devices updated regularly.
  • Be cautious clicking links or opening attachments.
  • Use a trusted VPN on public Wi-Fi networks.
  • Back up important data frequently.
